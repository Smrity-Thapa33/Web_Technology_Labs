Department of BSc CSIT

The Department of Computer Science and Information Technology (CSIT) focuses on providing high-quality education in computer science and technology. It's mission is to produce competent graduates with strong theoretical knowledge and practical skills in software development, networking, data analysis, designing and many more.

Key Activities:

  1. Software development projects
  2. Workshops and seminars
  3. Research in AI, ML, and Data Science
  4. Internship and industry collaborations
